About Us Rhodes Island Pharmaceuticals Inc. is a pharmaceutical company operating in the world. We specialize in developing and producing medicines to treat Oripathy, as well as providing support and rehabilitation for infected individuals. The Human Resources Department plays a key role in our organization, ensuring efficient personnel management, recruitment and training of Operators, and supporting their well-being and professional growth.

Operator Class Description
Caster: Users of Arts who inflict devastating magical damage, particularly effective against heavily armored targets. Casters trade durability for immense offensive power.
Core Deals Arts damage Single-target ranged Arts attacker
Splash Deals AOE Arts damage Ranged Arts attacker that deals area-of-effect/splash damage
Blast Deals AOE Arts damage in a long line Ranged Arts attacker that hits all targets in a linear range
Chain Attacks deal Arts damage and jump between 3/4 enemies. Each jump deals 15% less damage and inflicts a brief Slow Single-target ranged Arts attacker whose attack jumps between multiple targets (3 base, 4 Elite 2) with a 15% damage penalty in each jump and Slows them
Mech-Accord Controls a Drone to deal Arts damage to an enemy; When the Drone continuously attacks the same enemy, its damage will increase (up to 110% of the operator's ATK) Single-target ranged Arts attacker with a Drone that attacks in tandem with the Mech-Accord which deals low damage that increases on subsequent attacks against one target
Phalanx Normally does not attack, but has greatly increased DEF and RES; When skill is active, attacks deal AoE Arts damage Area-of-effect Arts attacker that only attacks during skill activations, dealing Arts damage to all targets in range, and has increased defenses outside of skill activations
Mystic Attacks deal Arts damage; When unable to find a target, attacks can be stored up and fired all at once (Up to 3 charges) Powerful single-target ranged Arts attacker that can "build up" attack charges when no enemies are in range to be released on the next attack
Attacks deal Arts damage, and can inflict Elemental damage Single-target ranged Arts attacker capable of inflicting Elemental damage on enemies
Deals Arts Damage; Can create Summons by defeating enemies, and can attack enemies blocked by these Summons Single-target ranged Arts attacker that obtains a summon when an enemy is defeated in range
Defender: Specialized in protection and endurance. Defenders absorb enemy attacks, hold choke points, and ensure the safety of Operators positioned behind them.
Guard: Versatile melee combatants capable of both offense and defense. Guards adapt to various situations, balancing survivability with high damage output.
Medic: Essential support Operators tasked with sustaining squad endurance. Medics restore HP, stabilize injured Operators, and ensure mission continuity.
Sniper: Long-range attackers focused on eliminating aerial units and priority threats. Snipers excel at precision and consistent ranged damage.
Specialist: Unique Operators with unconventional tactics. Specialists employ displacement, assassination, or area denial strategies to disrupt enemy formations.
Supporter: Specialists in tactical enhancement. Supporters provide buffs, debuffs, or summon units to manipulate the battlefield in favor of Rhodes Island.
Vanguard Responsible for establishing the frontline and securing initial deployment points. Vanguards are often the first to enter the battlefield, providing momentum and stability for the squad.
